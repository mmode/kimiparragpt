About me

Hi, my name is Kimi Parrag, and I am an experienced Personal Trainer based in London.

I have always been into keeping fit, particularly athletics and gymnastics. Working with clients, I effectively introduce exercise and nutritional behaviour into daily life to promote an overall better quality of life.
As a Personal Trainer I care, support and train a broad range of clients:
busy professionals, housewives (or husbands!), pre and post natal women, sports people, younger and older adults to name just a few.

I am part of the Active Ageing Programme. I am interested in balancing and strengthening the connection between the mind and body - making you fitter and healthier both physically and mentally.
